Anti-Drug game

I developed an anti-drug education video game using CORE, an open-source project by Unreal Engine. This game serves as an interactive tool to educate players about the dangers of drug abuse through immersive gameplay experiences. Leveraging the advanced capabilities of CORE, I meticulously crafted visually captivating environments and compelling storylines to effectively convey important messages. Through interactive scenarios and decision-making opportunities, players directly experience the consequences of drug abuse, fostering a deeper understanding of its impact. Educational content is seamlessly integrated into the gameplay, ensuring that players remain engaged while learning valuable information. Key features include detailed character design, dynamic environments, and a user-friendly interface optimized for immersive gameplay. This project showcases my proficiency in game development, and educational content creation, highlighting my ability to leverage technology for impactful social awareness initiatives and meaningful interactive experiences.
