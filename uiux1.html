CBDC Onilne Payment App

The platform integrates various financial services into a single system, offering streamlined management of payments, subscriptions, and insurance. It employs blockchain technology with advanced cryptographic methods like Zero-Knowledge Proofs and homomorphic encryption for secure and private transactions. Features include one-touch payment with biometric authentication, a chatbot for financial management, advanced QR payments, voice and hand gesture navigation, and a biometric authentication system. A comprehensive financial dashboard provides insights with interactive charts, helping users make informed decisions and control their spending.
