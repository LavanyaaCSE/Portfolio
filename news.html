News Wave

I developed a bilingual mobile application using Flutter, LSTM, and NLP, showcasing my expertise in mobile app development and advanced machine learning techniques. The app supports Tamil and English, categorizing news articles into various categories like sports, cinema, politics, and more, while also providing trending news, top feeds, and personalized recommendations based on user activity.
Leveraging LSTM for text processing and classification, the app ensures accurate categorization and seamless handling of both Tamil and English content. The recommendation system analyzes user engagement to suggest relevant news articles, enhancing user experience with tailored content. Users can explore trending stories and top feeds through a visually appealing and responsive interface designed to provide a smooth browsing experience.
Built with Flutter, the app delivers a fast and dynamic cross-platform experience with features like real-time updates and offline access. This project highlights my ability to integrate natural language processing with mobile development, creating an intelligent, user-centric application that bridges technology and language diversity to provide a powerful and intuitive news reading platform.
